# Unit 6 - Day 1: Graphs and Trees

## What is a graph?
  * A collection of nodes and edges

## What is a tree?
  * Type of graph
  * Collection of parent and child nodes connected by edges
  * Why use trees?
    * O(log n) insertion and sort time
